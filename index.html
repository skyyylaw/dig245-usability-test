Usability
Test

Tasks

  1. Enter zip code and 100 mile radius to search for adoptable "dog"
  2. Browse adorable kittens near you without entering zip code/location
  3. Find the adoption checklist
  4. Create an Account
  5. Discover "hairless" dog breeds

Results

Tasks/Performance Time (sec) Accuracy Emotional Response Recall
Task 1 7 perfect, no mistakes Engaged NA
Task 2 1 perfect, no mistakes Attention Decreased NA
Task 3 13 perfect, no mistakes Attention Decreased Good recall of homepage
Task 4 13 perfect, no mistakes Attention Decreased Good recall of homepage
Task 5 15 perfect, no mistakes attention decreased, amused by cute pics good recall of homepage, plus use of search engine

Discussion

Based on the results of the usability test, we can see that the user had no trouble navigating the site for the first time. Even though we noticed an attention decrease, the user was able to finish the tasks in less than half a minute without any mistake, while maintaining good recall of the content navigated previously. Aside from user feedback, the webpage layout accords to the conventions and there is an consistent use of contrast color. The content and design are relatively simplistic and easy to use, without redundant information.

However, it is noticed that the margin of different blocks of texts are not always consistent, and the background image of the search engine on homepage is of rather poor quality. Fixing these two issues could drastically improve the quality of the website and provide a better browsing experience.
